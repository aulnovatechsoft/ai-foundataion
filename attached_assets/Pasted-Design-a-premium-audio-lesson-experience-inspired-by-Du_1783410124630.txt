Design a premium audio lesson experience inspired by Duolingo, Coursera, and Audible. The player should feel modern, minimal, and distraction-free while remaining easy to use on mobile devices.

Requirements:

## Overall Experience
- Full-screen lesson player.
- Clean white background (light mode) with support for dark mode.
- Large lesson title and module name at the top.
- Display lesson progress (e.g., Lesson 3 of 20) and a progress bar.
- Smooth animations when opening and closing the player.
- Resume automatically from the last listening position.

## Audio Player UI
Create a professional audio player similar to Duolingo and Coursera with:

- Large Play/Pause button centered.
- Previous 10 seconds button.
- Next 10 seconds button.
- Seek/progress slider.
- Current playback time.
- Total audio duration.
- Remaining time.
- Animated waveform or subtle audio visualization while playing.
- Playback speed selector.

Supported playback speeds:
- 0.5x
- 0.75x
- 1x (Default)
- 1.25x
- 1.5x
- 1.75x
- 2x

## Reading Mode
Provide two learning modes:

1. Listen
2. Read Along

In Read Along mode:
- Display the lesson transcript below the player.
- Automatically highlight the current sentence as the audio plays.
- Smoothly scroll the transcript to keep the active sentence visible.
- Allow users to tap any sentence to jump the audio to that timestamp.
- Completed sentences become green.
- Current sentence is highlighted in blue.

## Lesson Controls
Include:
- Bookmark lesson.
- Download for offline listening.
- Sleep timer.
- Repeat current lesson.
- Repeat selected section.
- Skip intro.
- Jump to chapter.
- Continue where left off.

## Chapter Navigation
If the lesson contains multiple sections:
- Display chapter markers on the progress bar.
- Allow tapping a chapter to jump directly.
- Show completed chapters with green indicators.

## Completion Experience
When the audio reaches the end:
- Show a lesson completion animation.
- Display a green checkmark.
- Mark the lesson as completed.
- Unlock the next lesson automatically.
- Show a "Continue Learning" button.
- Allow replay at any time.

## Accessibility
- Large touch targets.
- VoiceOver/TalkBack support.
- Keyboard shortcuts for web.
- Adjustable text size.
- High contrast mode.
- Screen reader labels for every control.

## Smart Features
- Automatically save playback position.
- Continue playback after app restart.
- Background audio support.
- Lock-screen media controls.
- Bluetooth headset controls.
- Handle incoming phone calls gracefully.
- Offline playback for downloaded lessons.
- Buffering indicator during network delays.

## Micro Interactions
- Smooth button animations.
- Ripple effects on tap.
- Progress bar updates in real time.
- Gentle transition when changing playback speed.
- Animated waveform while audio is playing.
- Haptic feedback on supported devices.

## Visual Style
Inspired by:
- Duolingo
- Coursera
- Spotify
- Audible

Use:
- Rounded cards (16–20px radius)
- Soft shadows
- Minimal UI
- Material Design 3
- Premium typography
- Calm blue accent color
- Green completion color
- Clean spacing with generous padding

The overall experience should feel polished, modern, responsive, and premium, matching the quality of leading language-learning and online education apps.
